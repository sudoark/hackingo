Welcome to Hackingo

Hackingo Securities was founded on January 2023, by three individuals with the goal of assisting non-technical people and providing security services to you or your organisation.

Hackingo Securities is a leading provider of cyber security education and services. We specialize in offering comprehensive courses on penetration testing and other security-related topics. Our goal is to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills they need to protect themselves from cyber threats.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering the highest quality training and consulting services. We use cutting-edge techniques and tools to ensure that our students and clients stay ahead of the latest security threats. We also believe in encouraging more people to enter the cyber security field.

At Hackingo Securities, we are committed to helping our clients and students stay secure in the ever-changing digital landscape. Whether you're an individual looking to boost your skills or a company looking to improve your security posture, we have the resources to help you succeed.
